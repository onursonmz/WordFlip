Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

At WordFlip, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

WordFlip offers two modes of use: Guest Mode and Account-based Mode.

  • Guest Mode: If you use the app as a guest, all your data (words, scores) is stored locally on your device (localStorage). We do not collect or transmit any personal data in this mode.
  • Account Mode (Firebase Authentication): If you choose to sign in via Google, we use Firebase (a Google service) to manage your account. The following data is collected and stored securely in our database (Firebase Firestore) to provide synchronized access across your devices:
    • Your email address
    • Your display name
    • Your profile photo URL
    • Your saved words and performance data

2. Use of Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services which may collect information according to their own policies:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication and cloud data storage. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini (Generative AI): If you provide your own API key to use AI features, requests are sent to Google's AI services. We do not store your private API keys on our servers; they stay on your device.

3. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Profile section in the app, or by contacting us directly.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
